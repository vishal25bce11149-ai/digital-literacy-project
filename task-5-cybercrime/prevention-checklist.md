### Stay Safe Online — Checklist (For College Students in India)

###### Use strong, unique passwords for every account

###### Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) everywhere

###### Never share OTPs, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone

###### Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links

###### Verify job offers, internships, and emails before responding

###### Keep your apps and devices updated regularly

###### Use secure Wi-Fi; avoid public networks for sensitive tasks

Log out from shared or public computers

### UPI / Financial Safety Tips

###### Never scan QR codes to receive money (it’s always for paying)

###### Double-check UPI IDs and names before sending money Report Cybercrime Immediately

###### National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
